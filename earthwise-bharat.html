Who We Are

India's Trusted Partner in Environmental Excellence

Earthwise Bharat Pvt. Ltd. is a New Delhi-based environmental consultancy and compliance firm dedicated to transforming the way Indian businesses interact with our planet. We combine rigorous legal expertise, environmental science, and strategic ESG advisory to deliver end-to-end sustainability solutions.

As India accelerates its commitment to a net zero future under the Paris Agreement and aligns with global sustainability frameworks, businesses face mounting pressure — and opportunity — to lead with environmental responsibility. We are here to make that transition seamless, credible, and commercially advantageous.

From securing environmental clearances to designing net zero roadmaps and delivering ESG disclosures that meet global investor standards, Earthwise Bharat is the bridge between regulatory compliance and genuine sustainable impact.

Our Leadership

The Minds Behind Earthwise Bharat

Co-founded by two exceptional legal and environmental professionals, Earthwise Bharat combines courtroom-tested legal rigour with frontline environmental expertise to deliver solutions that truly matter.

👩‍⚖️
🥇 Gold Medalist
Ms. Shubhi Pandey
Co-Founder & Environmental Law Director

Ms. Shubhi Pandey is a Gold Medalist in LLM (Environment & Natural Resource Law) from TERI School of Advanced Studies, New Delhi, where she ranked first in her batch of 2024–25 — a distinction that reflects not only her academic brilliance but her passionate commitment to environmental justice.

A B.B.A. LL.B. graduate of Symbiosis Law School (SIU), Shubhi brings a rare blend of corporate law foundations and deep environmental specialisation. She has appeared and conducted research before the Supreme Court of India, the National Green Tribunal (NGT), and the National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission, and has worked with the UP Department of Environment, Forest & Climate Change — where she assisted in securing clearances from the Supreme Court, NGT, and the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, Government of India.

Shubhi has represented India at the UNFCCC COP 29 in Baku, Azerbaijan (2024), participated in the Summer School on Climate Justice & Law, and holds an ESG certification from The Nature's Eye. Her published research on outer space law and UN essay competition recognition reflect the breadth of her intellectual engagement. At Earthwise Bharat, she leads environmental compliance advisory, regulatory strategy, and ESG governance frameworks — bridging law, policy, and sustainable practice.

⚡ Energy Law Expert
Adv. Gurmehar Singh
Co-Founder & Corporate Compliance Director

Advocate Gurmehar Singh is a seasoned legal professional with a sharp focus on banking, finance, insolvency, and corporate compliance — disciplines that sit at the heart of India's sustainable transition. He holds an LLM from TERI School of Advanced Studies (2025) and a B.A. LL.B. Having worked with Khaitan & Khaitan on banking & finance matters, and having trained with leading firms including Luthra & Luthra, Singhania & Partners, and Vaish Associates, Gurmehar brings a sophisticated corporate legal lens to sustainability strategy — helping companies navigate complex regulatory landscapes while building responsible, future-ready business models.

What We Do

Comprehensive Sustainability Services

From legal compliance to transformative ESG strategies, our services cover the full spectrum of what modern Indian businesses need to grow responsibly and sustainably.

⚖️

Environmental Compliance

We guide businesses through India's complex environmental regulatory framework — ensuring you meet every legal obligation while avoiding costly penalties and reputational risk.

  • Environmental clearance applications (MOEF&CC & State PCBs)
  • Consent to Establish & Consent to Operate
  • Forest land diversion & tree-cutting permissions
  • Wildlife & biodiversity compliance
  • NGT and High Court representation
  • Compliance audits under EPA 1986, FCA 1980, WPA 1972
🌿

Environmental Consultancy

Strategic environmental advisory tailored to your project, sector, and region — from Environmental Impact Assessments to biodiversity management plans and pollution control strategies.

  • Environmental Impact Assessments (EIA)
  • Environmental Management Plans (EMP)
  • Site-specific ecological baseline studies
  • Pollution load analysis & mitigation strategies
  • Climate risk and resilience assessments
  • Green building & infrastructure advisory
🏭

Net Zero Campus Transformation

We design and implement end-to-end net zero roadmaps for corporate campuses, manufacturing units, and office complexes — turning your operations into a showcase of sustainable excellence.

  • Carbon footprint mapping & GHG inventories
  • Renewable energy transition planning
  • Zero waste operations design
  • Water positivity strategies
  • Scope 1, 2 & 3 emissions reduction plans
  • Net zero certification support
📊

ESG Advisory & Reporting

Comprehensive Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) strategy and disclosure services that satisfy global investor expectations, SEBI's BRSR mandate, and international reporting frameworks.

  • SEBI BRSR (Business Responsibility & Sustainability Reporting)
  • GRI Standards reporting & alignment
  • ESG materiality assessments
  • Investor ESG due diligence support
  • Supply chain ESG risk mapping
  • ESG ratings advisory (MSCI, Sustainalytics, CDP)
⚡

Energy Sector Sustainability

Specialist sustainability consulting for India's LPG, hydrocarbon, and energy sectors — bridging the transition from conventional fuels to clean energy with compliance, strategy, and legal rigour.

  • LPG sector environmental compliance
  • Clean energy transition roadmaps
  • Hydrocarbon facility compliance audits
  • OALP & petroleum regulatory compliance
  • Energy efficiency certifications
  • Flaring reduction & emissions strategies

Green Finance & Policy Advisory

  • Carbon credit & offset advisory
